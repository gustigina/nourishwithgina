About

Gusti Gina

Actress · Writer · Content Creator

I didn't learn to cook in a kitchen that smelled like warmth.

I learned to cook because I had to. Because sometimes survival looks like figuring out how to feed yourself and the people you love, on a budget, with what's available, when you're already exhausted.

I grew up carrying a lot. I raised my siblings. I smiled for cameras before I knew how to rest. I built a career out of storytelling, books, films, a million strangers watching me on their phones, while quietly falling apart in ways nobody could see.

Food was the first thing that made me slow down.

Not fancy food. Not recipes from culinary school. Just, what if I made something that actually nourished me? What if eating could be an act of care instead of an afterthought?

That question changed everything.

NourishWithGina is where I keep what I've figured out. Recipes that are clean but never boring. Food that works with your body, not against it. The science behind why certain ingredients make you feel alive, and the honest truth that pleasure and healing are not opposites.

I'm Gusti Gina. Actress. Writer. Content creator. Indonesian, Banjarese, and deeply proud of both.

This space is for every woman who has spent years taking care of everyone else and is finally learning, slowly, imperfectly, to take care of herself first.

You deserve food that tastes like love.
