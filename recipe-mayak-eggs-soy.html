Mayak Eggs — Soy Sauce

⏱ 10 min + overnight
👤 10 eggs
🔥 160 kcal (2 eggs)
💪 14g protein

Ingredients

The eggs
  • 10 eggs
The marinade sauce
  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 5 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 1 chili, sliced (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1–1.5 tbsp white vinegar or lime juice
  • 3–4 thin ginger slices

How to Make It

  1. 01

    Boil the eggs. Bring water to a boil. Gently lower eggs in with a spoon. Timer: exactly 7 minutes.

  2. 02

    Ice bath. Remove eggs immediately to a bowl of ice water + cold water. Rest 5 minutes. This stops cooking and makes peeling easy.

  3. 03

    Peel under water. Peel each egg while submerged. The water gets under the membrane and the shell comes off cleanly.

  4. 04

    Mix the sauce. Combine all marinade ingredients and stir well.

  5. 05

    Marinate. Add eggs to container or ziplock bag. Pour sauce over. If using ziplock, remove all air — the sauce will cover every egg even if there is not much. Minimum 4 hours. Overnight is best.

🌿 Chef's Note from Gina

"7 minutes exactly — not 8, not 6. The jammy golden yolk is everything. Peel under water — so much easier. Ziplock trick: remove all air and the sauce covers every egg even if you do not have much marinade."
