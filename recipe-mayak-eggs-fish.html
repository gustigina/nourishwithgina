Mayak Eggs — Fish Sauce

⏱ 10 min + overnight
👤 10 eggs
🔥 160 kcal (2 eggs)
💪 14g protein

Ingredients

The eggs
  • 10 eggs
The marinade sauce
  • 5 tbsp fish sauce
  • 5 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 1 chili, sliced (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 2–3 thin lemon slices + juice of ½ lemon
  • 3–4 thin ginger slices

How to Make It

  1. 01

    Boil the eggs. Bring water to a boil. Gently lower eggs in with a spoon. Timer: exactly 7 minutes.

  2. 02

    Ice bath. Remove eggs immediately to a bowl of ice water + cold water. Rest 5 minutes.

  3. 03

    Peel under water. Peel each egg while submerged — the water gets under the membrane and the shell comes off cleanly.

  4. 04

    Mix the fish sauce marinade. Combine fish sauce, water, sesame oil, honey, minced garlic, spring onions, lemon slices and juice, ginger, and sesame seeds. Stir well. Do not add extra salt.

  5. 05

    Marinate overnight. Add eggs to container or ziplock. Pour marinade over. Minimum 4 hours in fridge — overnight is best. The fish sauce penetrates deeper and the flavor is more complex.

🌿 Chef's Note from Gina

"Fish sauce version is more complex and umami-forward. Do not add extra salt — fish sauce is already very salty. Use lemon, not vinegar — fish sauce and lemon is a classic Southeast Asian combo that just works."
