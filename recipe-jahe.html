Rebusan Jahe + Madu Lemon

⏱ 15 min
👤 2 cups
🔥 30 kcal
💪 0g protein

Ingredients

What you need
  • Fresh ginger, sliced thin (3–4 cm piece)
  • 1 tbsp honey (added after, not boiled)
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Water from gallon jug (not tap)

How to Make It

  1. 01

    Start ginger in cold water. Add sliced ginger to the water before it boils. This allows the gingerol to slowly extract into the water as temperature rises.

  2. 02

    Boil 10–15 minutes. Let it simmer until the water turns golden. The stronger the color, the stronger the medicine.

  3. 03

    Cool slightly. Remove from heat. Let it come down to warm — not boiling. If you can comfortably hold the cup, it is ready for honey.

  4. 04

    Add honey and lemon. Stir in honey until dissolved. Squeeze in lemon juice. Drink slowly while warm.

🌿 Chef's Note from Gina

"Use gallon water, not tap. Add honey only when warm, not boiling — or the enzymes die and it is just sugar. Ginger goes in before boiling so it steeps properly from the start. This saved my voice when I was sick."
