The science behind the plate

Food is medicine.

Psychonutrition is the study of how food affects your mind. Every ingredient I choose — the leafy greens, the fermented foods, the good fats — is picked not just for flavor, but for what it does to your brain chemistry.

Serotonin, dopamine, cortisol — all of it is shaped by what you eat. When you nourish your body right, you nourish your whole self. That's what these recipes are about.
