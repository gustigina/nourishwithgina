Marinated Sesame Tuna

⏱ 20 min
👤 1–2 servings
🔥 130 kcal
💪 27g protein

Ingredients

The tuna + marinade
  • 200g tuna steak
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1.5 tsp sesame oil
  • Juice of ½ lime
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika (optional)

How to Make It

  1. 01

    Mix the marinade. Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, lime juice, and honey. Stir well.

  2. 02

    Marinate the tuna. Coat the tuna in marinade and let it rest 15–20 minutes. Don't rush this — the lime juice starts to work on the protein.

  3. 03

    Remove and keep the marinade. Take the tuna out. Keep every drop of the marinade in the bowl — you will need it.

  4. 04

    Sear in a hot dry pan. Very hot, no oil. 90 seconds on first side — do not touch.

  5. 05

    Flip. 90 seconds on the other side.

  6. 06

    The secret finish. Pour the reserved marinade over the tuna in the pan. Toss for 30 seconds until it glazes and caramelizes. Remove immediately. This is the moment.

🌿 Chef's Note from Gina

"The reserved marinade poured at the end is the secret. It creates a glossy caramelized sauce in seconds. Anders invented this move and it changed everything."
