Air Fryer Cherry Tomato

⏱ 12 min
👤 2 servings
🔥 45 kcal
💪 1g protein

Ingredients

Everything you need
  • 200g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Dried oregano, to taste

How to Make It

  1. 01

    Wash and dry. Rinse the cherry tomatoes. Pat dry — they roast better when not wet.

  2. 02

    Season. Toss with olive oil, salt, and dried oregano until coated.

  3. 03

    Air fryer 180°C, 10–12 minutes. They are done when they burst open. That is the signal.

  4. 04

    No air fryer? Oven at 200°C for 15 minutes. Same result, slightly longer.

  5. 05

    Use as a topping. Scatter over a protein bowl with Korean Beef Bulgogi or Seared Tuna, add over pasta, or eat straight from the tray.

🌿 Chef's Note from Gina

"When they burst open and the juice caramelizes slightly — that is the moment. Goes on everything: pasta, bowls, or eaten straight from the tray. I make a batch every week."
