Pan Seared Tuna

⏱ 15 min
👤 1 serving
🔥 180 kcal
💪 38g protein

Ingredients

The tuna
  • 180–200g frozen tuna (thawed)
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp olive oil

How to Make It

  1. 01

    Thaw properly. Place tuna (still in packaging or ziplock) in cold water for 20–30 minutes. Do not use warm water — it cooks the outer layer unevenly.

  2. 02

    Pat completely dry. This is the most critical step. Use paper towel, press firmly, repeat. Dry = sear. Wet = steam. They are completely different results.

  3. 03

    Season. Coat with smoked paprika, salt, pepper, and a thin layer of olive oil. Rub it in with your hands.

  4. 04

    Screaming hot pan. No extra oil. High heat. The pan must be very hot before the tuna touches it.

  5. 05

    Sear 2 minutes. Place tuna, do not touch, do not press. Let it sear undisturbed for 2 full minutes.

  6. 06

    Flip once. 1.5–2 minutes on the other side. Remove. Pink inside is perfect — that is how tuna is meant to be eaten.

🌿 Chef's Note from Gina

"Dry = sear. Wet = steam. Pat it like you mean it. Pink inside is not raw — tuna is meant to be served medium. Trust the process."
